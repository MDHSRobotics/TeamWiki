Target description:
A vision target is a pair of 5½ in. (~14 cm) long by 2 in. (~5 cm) wide strips of 3M 8830 Scotchlite Reflective Material. Strips are angled toward each other at ~14.5° and such that there’s an 8-in. (~20 cm) gap at their closest points.

Measurements are to bumper cutout on Cargo Ship and HP station (not the target) and to plastic face on rocket.
